Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ResumeForge and our free resume builder.

Is ResumeForge completely free?
Yes, ResumeForge is 100% free and open-source. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. All templates and features are available at no cost.

Are the resume templates ATS-friendly?
Absolutely! All our resume templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. They use clean formatting and proper structure to ensure your resume passes through automated screening systems used by employers.

Do I need to create an account to use ResumeForge?
No account is required. Simply choose a template and start building your resume immediately. Your data stays in your browser for maximum privacy and security.

Can I download my resume as a PDF?
Yes, you can download your resume as a high-quality PDF file instantly. The PDF format ensures your resume looks professional and maintains its formatting across all devices and platforms.

Is my data secure?
Your privacy is our priority. All your resume data is stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server. You have complete control over your information.

Can I edit my resume after downloading?
Yes! Your resume data is saved in your browser, so you can return anytime to make edits and download updated versions of your resume.
